02 Brody: Summer Program for Future Doctors (SPFD) "College/Post-College: must have completed basic prerequisite courses for medical school including 2 semesters of biology chemistry and physics" Takes place in Greenville; open to NC residents Office of Medical Education @ BSOM https://medicine.ecu.edu/medicaleducation/spfd/

"Students on the pre-med track strengthen knowledge of the basic sciences enhance their critical thinking skills gain a better understanding of the application and admissions process to medical school and assess their ability to successfully handle the demands of medical school. Intensive challenging educational summer program that simulates the pedagogical style and demands of medical school. Brody accepts approximately 30 participants each summer for the 8-week program. Must be an NC resident students of minority disadvantaged and non-traditional backgrounds who meet other requirements are given priority. "
